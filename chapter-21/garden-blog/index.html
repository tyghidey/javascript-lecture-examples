My Plans for the New Flowerbed

For now, since it's autumn, I will be focusing on planting crocus, hyacinth, daffodil, and tulip bulbs for spring blooming. In the spring I will plant some lilies for summer blooming.

Tomorrow's Tasks

  1. Lay out hyacinth in clusters of 3
  2. Lay out crocuses in clusters of 6
  3. Lay out tulips and daffodils in a staggered pattern around clusters
  4. Plant hyacinth, tulips & daffodils 4" deep without disturbing unplanted crocus bulbs
  5. Add 2" topsoil, covering crocus bulbs without turning them over
  6. Cover entire flowerbed with 3" of mulch
  7. Water thoroughly without disturbing mulch
